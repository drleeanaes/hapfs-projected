Methodology
Salary: Yr1 HK$127,700 · Yr2 HK$132,275 · Yr3 HK$137,085 · Yr4 HK$142,010 · Yr5 HK$147,125 · Yr6+ compounded 2.5% p.a.
HA employer contribution: 15% of monthly basic salary — no cap, no employee contribution required under HAPFS.
Fund return: HAPFS Global Equity Fund 9.11% p.a. CAGR (derived from 10-year cumulative return of 139.6%, factsheet as at 31/3/2026). Applied as monthly compounding (0.7592%/month).
Vesting: 0% if credited service <3 years · 30% at 3 yrs · 40% at 4 yrs · 50% at 5 yrs · 60% at 6 yrs · 70% at 7 yrs · 80% at 8 yrs · 90% at 9 yrs · 100% at 10+ years (Appendix III, HA 13 March 2025).
On leaving: the vested portion is claimable. The forfeited portion returns to HA. Minimum MPF Benefits (MMB) are preserved separately under MPF Ordinance regardless of vesting. All figures are gross (pre-tax). Past performance does not guarantee future results. Not financial advice.
